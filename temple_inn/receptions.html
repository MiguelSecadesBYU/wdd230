Wedding Receptions

We know how important the day of your eternal marriage is to you, which is why at the Temple Inn & Suites we will take care of everything you need, both regarding your stay and your wedding reception. Taking care of even the smallest detail, following the highest quality standards, so that everything goes perfectly and you don't have to worry about anything other than enjoying YOUR day to the fullest. We will prepare all the decoration, the music, we will receive the guests and we will prepare the most exquisite menu, at the height of a day as important as this. At The Temple Inn & Suites, we are committed to an event as important as the sealing, and our commitment is expressed in our dedication to every detail to make this day unforgettable.
