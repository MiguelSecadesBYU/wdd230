Typography

Alegreya SC

For headers, our website will use the "Alegreya SC" font in its "Extra-bold 800" style.

Roboto

In the paragraphs, the font used will be the "roboto" font in its "light 300" style.

The choice of these sources is mainly due to two reasons. On the one hand, they are very widespread fonts, and therefore, it is easier and faster to load these fonts on the different devices of our visitors. And on the other hand, they are clear fonts with very good legibility, something especially important especially on mobile devices due to their small size.
