Target Audience

Visitors to the website of the Gijón Chamber of Commerce would be mainly of two types. On the one hand, there would be the owners of small and medium-sized companies who want to both make their services known among the citizens of the city of Gijón and take advantage of the benefits of belonging to the Gijón Chamber of Commerce. Benefits would range from collaborative agreements between the different members of the Chamber, fairs where they can show their products and services, and other very succulent benefits for small and medium-sized companies in Gijón.

On the other hand, another main group of visitors to our website would be citizens of Gijón, interested in finding out about the shops and companies in their city, to be able to buy or contract their services without having to travel outside the city.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Mariano Fernández, manager of MerkaPlus enters our website interested in discovering the benefits of belonging to the Chamber of Commerce. To do this, go to the home page of our website and from the menu click on the "Join" button, where you will be shown both the benefits of membership in the Chamber of Commerce and a form to request said membership. After reviewing the benefits, Mariano is satisfied with them and fills out the form, to later click on the "submit" button.

Scenario 2

Luisa Díaz needs to buy new shoes for her son since his current shoes are very worn. He enters the website of the Gijón Chamber of Commerce, intending to look for a good shoe store near his home. To do this, go to the Gijón Chamber of Commerce website from your mobile phone and click on "Directory" in the menu, where you check the list of local businesses until you find the shoe store "Footprints". There he writes down his telephone number and his address to go look at his footwear catalog.

Scenario 3

Antonio López needs to obtain some demographic data of the city of Gijón for an assignment from the University. To obtain this information, check various sources, one of these sources is the website of the Gijón Chamber of Commerce where, after entering said website, click on the Discover section of the menu, and there you obtain different historical and demographic data of the city, which They are very helpful for your assignment.
